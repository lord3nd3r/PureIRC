Get Connected

How to Join PureIRC

Choose the method that works best for you. All connections are free.

Web Client

No install needed. Connect instantly in your browser.

  1. 1 Click the "Open Web Client" button below
  2. 2 Enter your desired nickname
  3. 3 Join any channel by typing /join #channelname

IRC Client

Use a dedicated client like HexChat, mIRC, or WeeChat.

  1. 1 Download and install your preferred IRC client
  2. 2 Set the server to irc.pureirc.com, port 6667 (or 6697 for SSL)
  3. 3 Choose a nickname and connect

Mobile

Stay connected on the go with a mobile IRC app.

  1. 1 Install LimeChat (iOS) or Revolution IRC (Android)
  2. 2 Add a new network: irc.pureirc.com
  3. 3 Set port to 6667, or 6697 for SSL

Network Guidelines

Rules & Conduct

PureIRC thrives on mutual respect. These rules keep our community safe and enjoyable for everyone.

Be Respectful

  • Treat all users with dignity and respect, regardless of background or opinion.
  • No harassment, bullying, threats, or personal attacks toward any user.
  • Discrimination based on race, gender, religion, nationality, or sexuality is strictly prohibited.
  • Disagreements are fine — be constructive, not hostile.

Network Safety

  • No flooding, spamming, or excessive repetition in any channel.
  • Do not share malware, exploit scripts, or harmful links.
  • Clones, bots, and automated connections require prior approval from staff.
  • Advertising other networks or services without permission is not allowed.

Content Standards

  • NSFW content must remain in age-gated channels designated for such material.
  • No sharing of illegal content of any kind, ever.
  • Doxxing — publishing private information about others — results in immediate permanent ban.
  • Channel topics and names must be appropriate and not misleading.

Enforcement

  • Warnings are issued for first-time minor violations at operator discretion.
  • Repeat or serious offenses result in temporary or permanent network bans.
  • IRC Operators (IRCops) have final authority on all network matters.
  • Appeals can be submitted by messaging an IRCop directly.

By connecting to PureIRC you agree to abide by these rules. Ignorance of the rules is not an excuse.
